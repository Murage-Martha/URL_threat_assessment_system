URL Threat Analysis System

What does this tool do?

Our safety scanner acts like a digital security guard, carefully checking every website link you're curious about. It gives you a simple, easy-to-understand safety rating so you can browse with confidence.

How we protect you

We use multiple layers of protection to keep you safe:

  • Smart Pattern Detection: Our system looks for suspicious signs in website addresses
  • AI Protection: Advanced artificial intelligence predicts potential threats
  • Global Threat Database: Checks against worldwide lists of dangerous sites
  • Real-Time Updates: Always learning about new threats as they emerge

Why this matters

Every day, thousands of people fall victim to fake websites. Our scanner helps you spot these dangers before they can cause harm.

Your Safety Checklist

Do:

  • Always hover over links to preview the destination URL before clicking to avoid hidden malicious sites
  • Verify the padlock icon and 'https://' in your browser's address bar to ensure encrypted, secure connections
  • Scan any suspicious links with our system before visiting to detect potential phishing or malware threats

Don't:

  • Click unexpected links in emails or messages, even from known contacts, as accounts may be compromised
  • Enter passwords, credit card details, or personal information on sites without verified security certificates
  • Believe offers promising free gifts, huge discounts, or prizes that require immediate action or information
